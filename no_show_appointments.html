Introduction¶

This worksheet will explore the dataset of medical appointments in Brazil and is focused on the question of whether patients show up for their appointment. the dataset contains 14 columns including the patients no-show status, it consists of 110527 entries. we will explore the data, wrangle, clean, and try to understand the data using summary statistics and visualizations, as couple of questions and try to find answers. The first question will be focusing on the period between scheduling day and appointment day and if this affects the patient no-show status. The second questions will be regarding if elements such as [Scholarship, Age, sms_received] affect their status.

Data Wrangling¶

In this section we will be importing the necessary libraries and requirements, load the data and check for any duplicates, Null, nonscience data, and have an overview of the data and prepare it for the next step.
